Welcome to Valorant

In this free-to-play 5v5 team-based game, you'll navigate a world where precise gunplay meets unique agent abilities. Team up, strategize, and engage in thrilling
matches as you plant or defuse the Spike. Valorant combines the best of traditional FPS gameplay with strategic character abilities, offering an exhilarating
experience where every move matters.

Learn the Agents

Agents are the playable characters in VALORANT with each agent serving
as a different class with four abilities. Find out more about the agents
in the Agents page.

>

Know the Maps

There are many different maps in Valorant each with unique aspects and layouts
where you would need to adapt your tactics to navigate these dynamic environments
causing matches to have depth.

Explore the Gamemodes

From fun short minigames with unique aspects to competitive matches where you
earn ranked rating, in Valorant there are many different gamemodes to peak
your interest.
